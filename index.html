FAQ

  • What happens after the shoot? Latest 24 hours after the shoot, I will send you a link to the images. You choose the photos you want to get retouched and I will deliver each of them in black&white and colour within 2 working days.
  • How should I pay? As a confirmation of booking I will need to receive a £20 deposit from you. This amount will be deducted form the final payment. The easiest way is bank transfer directly to my account. Alternatively you can pay in cash on the day of the shoot.
  • Why are you taking a deposit? For my work I must plan ahead and I need to be sure you will turn up to the shoot. However, a session can be re-booked up to 2 days before the shoot.
  • What should I bring with me? A few changes of tops with different necklines without contrast patterns or eye-catching details. A combination of dark and light clothes.
  • Do you make prints yourself? No, I don't but I can recommend a very good and affordable service: 10x8prints.
  • What does ‘retouching’ of an image mean? Basic blemishes and temporary spots are removed to help look your best. It takes around 30 minutes per image.
